Celestial

Computer Science 4 Honors Final Project



Project Overview

Celestial is a 2D space-themed story game inspired by Mario, with 3 levels, including a final boss battle. The story follows Luna, who embarks on a journey across the Milky Way to rescue her love, Sunny, who has been imprisoned by the space warden Uranus. The game includes enemies like asteroids, gassy planets, and other space-themed obstacles, with different objectives for each level. My Contributions

In developing *Celestial*, I created a game that explored themes of love and adventure, featuring engaging mechanics, diverse enemies, and challenges. I focused on an immersive experience with pixel art and sound, using Godot and GDScript to implement game logic efficiently. Targeting a younger audience with a message of acceptance, I aimed to deliver a game that balanced creativity, design, and project management.
