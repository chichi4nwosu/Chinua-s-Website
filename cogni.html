CogniFit 2023

Congerssional App Challenge



Project Overview

Our app, CogniFit, is dedicated to enhancing the cognitive health of the elderly, offering a variety of engaging brain-training games and daily tasks designed to keep seniors mentally active and sharp. With features like reflex challenges, memory exercises, and personalized well-being surveys, CogniFit promotes a fulfilling and active lifestyle for older adults.

My Contributions

As one of the developers on the CogniFit team, I played a pivotal role in creating three key features of the app. I focused on implementing the melody game, the word unscramble game, and the reflex challenge game, ensuring they were both enjoyable and beneficial for our users.






Congressional App Challenge

June 2023 - February 2024

The Congressional App Challenge provides an incredible platform for students across the nation to showcase their creativity and technical skills. Participating in this challenge allowed our team to innovate with CogniFit, an app designed to enhance cognitive health for the elderly. It was a rewarding experience to present our solution, highlighting how technology can positively impact the lives of seniors by keeping them mentally active and engaged. We're proud to have contributed to this competition and look forward to continuing our journey of using technology for meaningful advancements in healthcare and well-being.
