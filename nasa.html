The Lunar Leaper 2024

NASA High School Aerospace Scholars



Project Overview

Participating in the NASA Moonshot program, our team designed a lunar hopper to expand surface exploration capabilities on the Moon. This innovative vehicle enables navigation across challenging lunar terrains, enhancing resource discovery and contributing to the broader objectives of NASA's Artemis program.

My Contributions

As the subtopic manager for Autonomous Manufacturing, Excavation, and Construction, I played a crucial role in developing strategies for lunar surface operations. My contributions focused on optimizing the lunar hopper's functionality and coordinating efforts to integrate it into the broader mission framework.








NASA High School Aerospace Scholars

September 2023 - June 2024

NASA High School Aerospace Scholars is an immersive STEM program designed to inspire and engage high school students in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Through hands-on learning experiences, interactive challenges, and mentorship from NASA scientists and engineers, HAS participants delve into real-world aerospace projects and gain insight into NASA's missions and career opportunities. This prestigious program equips students with critical thinking skills, teamwork abilities, and a deep appreciation for space exploration, preparing them for future academic and professional pursuits in STEM fields. By fostering a passion for innovation and discovery, NASA HAS empowers the next generation of leaders to make significant contributions to space science and exploration.
