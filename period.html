Period AI 2023

New York Junior Academy of Science



Project Overview

The "Period AI" project aims to leverage natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning to create an AI chatbot that educates users about menstruation. This initiative addresses misconceptions by providing accurate information in a user-friendly manner, promoting informed conversations and dispelling myths surrounding periods.

My Contributions

As the lead software engineer, I orchestrated the development of the Period AI website using my expertise in web development, AI, and natural language processing. My responsibilities included overseeing a team of six, ensuring the completion of over 300 informative answers related to women's health issues, and integrating these into a cohesive platform accessible to users.








New York Junior Academy of Science

August 2023 - April 2024

The New York Junior Academy of Sciences (NYJAS) is a prestigious organization dedicated to nurturing young scientists and fostering a passion for scientific inquiry. As a part of the New York Academy of Sciences, NYJAS provides a platform for middle and high school students to engage in cutting-edge research, collaborate with peers and mentors, and present their findings at regional and national competitions. Through hands-on projects, workshops, and mentoring programs, NYJAS cultivates critical thinking skills, scientific literacy, and leadership qualities among its members. By encouraging curiosity and innovation, NYJAS prepares students to tackle global challenges and make meaningful contributions to the scientific community.
