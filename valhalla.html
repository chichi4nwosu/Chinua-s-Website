Valhalla 2024

Computer Science 3 Honors Spring Final Project



Project Overview

In our project "Valhalla," inspired by classic games like Pokemon and Mario, players embark on an epic journey to conquer monsters and traverse a dynamic map. The objective is straightforward yet challenging: defeat all monsters to emerge victorious. This text-based game merges nostalgia with innovation, offering players a compelling adventure experience.

My Contributions

As part of the Valhalla team, I spearheaded the creation of the game's storyline and power-up mechanics. Additionally, I played a pivotal role in developing core functionalities such as Player.cs, Monster.cs, and Map.cs. By blending creativity with technical expertise, I ensured that each aspect of the game contributes to a seamless and engaging gameplay experience.








Computer Science 3 Honors Final Project

May 2024

Computer Science 3 Honors is a challenging equivalent to a college-level class, designed to immerse students in advanced computer science concepts and rigorous programming exercises. Serving as a "weed-out" course, it pushes students to their limits, testing their ability to apply theoretical knowledge to practical coding challenges under pressure. Despite its demanding nature, the course fosters a supportive environment where I've felt comfortable exploring new coding techniques and collaborating with peers. This experience has not only deepened my understanding of programming languages like Python, Java, and C#, but also sharpened my problem-solving skills and ability to thrive in dynamic, team-oriented settings.
